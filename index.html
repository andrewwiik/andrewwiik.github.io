About Us

Creatix is a team of two designers and one developer, who want to bring the most simplistic tweak to the Jailbreak community to improve the end user's experience on their Apple Device.

Making our debut with BatteryIcon, ShowMeMore and MoreRecent, Creatix will be the team that will bring changes to the community by releasing the most requested tweaks and designing so that they feel native to iOS.

Often times a jailbreak developer has a great idea, but they don't know how to execute their idea in a way that will provide useful functionality to the end user while still feeling native to iOS.

That's where Creatix comes in, because we have desginers and a developer on our team we are able to span that gap to provide tweaks that feel native to iOS but also provide functionality to the end user that wasn't there before.

Our Development Process

1. Listen

We listen to what the community thinks would be a great tweak.

2. Design

We take a tweak request and design a concept that feels native to iOS.

3. Develop

Our developer takes the concept and makes it into tweak.

4. Fix

We listen to the feedback you give to make sure the tweak is bug-free.
